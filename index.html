The Art of Making a Digital Art Community

How we made one online world for many types of artists and art lovers

The Problem

Redesign an underused site to create new social opportunities. In our concept project for Hunter’s Point Shipyard Association (HPSA), which runs a colony of 300 artists, our UX team focused on two main goals: exposure of the artists’ work and ways to engage the artists and the public.

The Solution

The site has to be special in its own right to draw people to it. Our idea to address these goals is to redesign the HPSA Web site with:

  • A site that has current, compelling content, much of it from the artists themselves
  • Innovative ways for the artists and public to interact.
